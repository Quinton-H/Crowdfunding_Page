About this project

The Mastercraft Bamboo Monitor Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing height. Placing your monitor at eye level has the potenial to improve your posture and make you more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand.

Featuring artisan craftsman, the simplicity of design creates extra space below your computer to allow notepads, pens, and USB stickes to be stored under the stand.
